Postgraduate Thesis - Explainable AI for Medical Diagnosis

My thesis quantifies the effectiveness of explainable AI methods, including SHAP, LIME, and Integrated Gradients, for skin cancer diagnosis models. This research aims to improve the transparency of AI models in medical applications.

  • Technologies: TensorFlow, Python, SHAP, LIME
  • Impact: Increased trust and transparency in medical AI applications.

Humor Genome Project

As part of the Humor Genome Project, I worked on analyzing humor using machine learning to create humor classifiers and recommenders. The project explored linguistic patterns to enhance the accuracy of humor-based recommendations.

  • Technologies: Python, TensorFlow, NLP, Recommender Systems

Bio Robotics Human Modeling Lab

During my time at the Bio Robotics Lab, I worked on creating a haptic lift assist device for General Motors. This project aimed to improve industrial assembly processes by developing a more intuitive and safer human-machine interaction model.

  • Technologies: LabVIEW, Robotics, Haptic Devices
